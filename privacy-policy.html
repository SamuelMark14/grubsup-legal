Grubs Up

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Grubs Up collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the Grubs Up mobile app.

Grubs Up is a meal-planning and recipe-management app designed to help users organise recipes, build meal plans, create shopping lists, and import recipe content for personal use.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Account information, such as your email address and account identifier, when you sign up or log in.
  • Profile and household information, such as your display name, household name, household size, allergy preferences, exclusions, and pantry staples.
  • Recipe and planning content, including recipes, ingredients, meal plans, shopping lists, favourites, and imported recipe drafts.
  • Uploaded files and images, such as recipe photos, recipe cover images, screenshots, and documents that you choose to upload for import or attachment.
  • Feedback and bug reports, including the message you submit and limited app context such as app version, platform, signed-in email, user ID, and household ID.

How We Use Information

We use this information to:

  • create and manage your account;
  • store and sync your recipes, meal plans, and shopping lists;
  • power recipe import, OCR, and formatting flows;
  • generate shopping lists and household planning features;
  • respond to support requests, bug reports, and feedback; and
  • maintain and improve the app.

Recipe Photos, OCR, and Imports

When you upload recipe photos, screenshots, or documents for import, Grubs Up processes that content to extract recipe information and prefill editable recipe fields.

Depending on the import path used by the app, processing may happen on-device or through backend services that support Grubs Up’s recipe import functionality.

Storage and Service Providers

Grubs Up uses service providers to operate the app, including hosting, authentication, database, file storage, and recipe import processing providers. These providers process data on behalf of Grubs Up to support app functionality.

Sharing of Information

Grubs Up does not sell your personal information. We may share information only when necessary to provide app functionality, operate the service through our providers, comply with legal obligations, or protect the app and its users.

Data Retention

We retain account and app data for as long as your account remains active, or as needed to provide the service and comply with legal obligations. If you request deletion, we will delete or anonymise eligible data within a reasonable period, unless retention is required for legal, security, or fraud-prevention reasons.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect user data in transit and at rest. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices

You can edit recipe, household, and planning content inside the app. You can also contact Grubs Up to request account deletion using the instructions on the account deletion page.

Children

Grubs Up is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we may update the date on this page and revise the policy text accordingly.
