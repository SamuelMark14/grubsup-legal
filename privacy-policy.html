Grubs Up

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Grubs Up collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the Grubs Up mobile app.

Grubs Up is a meal-planning and recipe-management app designed to help users organise recipes, build meal plans, create shopping lists, and import recipe content for personal use.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Account information, such as your email address and account identifier, when you sign up or sign in.
  • Profile and household information, such as your display name, household name, household size, allergies, exclusions, and pantry staples.
  • User content, such as recipes, ingredients, steps, cover photos, meal plans, shopping lists, favourites, and notes you create or edit in the app.
  • Imported content, such as recipe URLs, pasted text, uploaded documents, and recipe photos or screenshots that you choose to import.
  • Shared recipe information, if you choose to enable recipe sharing through a share link or token.
  • Support and bug report information, such as feedback messages, bug reports, app version, platform details, household context, and signed-in email address when you send a report through the app.
  • Technical information, such as device platform, app version, import-processing metadata, and basic logs needed to operate and troubleshoot the service.

How We Use Your Information

We use information collected through Grubs Up to:

  • create and manage your account;
  • save and sync your recipes, meal plans, and shopping lists;
  • process recipe imports from text, files, and images;
  • store and display recipe cover photos and other content you choose to upload;
  • enable optional recipe sharing features you choose to use;
  • respond to bug reports, support requests, and user feedback;
  • improve app performance, reliability, and user experience; and
  • maintain security, prevent abuse, and troubleshoot technical issues.

Photos, Files, and OCR

If you choose to import a recipe from a photo, screenshot, or file, Grubs Up will access only the items you select. These files may be processed to extract text and structure recipe content for review before saving.

On supported Android devices, some image text recognition may be processed on-device. In other cases, recipe import content may be processed by our backend services and AI service providers to extract and structure the recipe text.

How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share information with trusted service providers that help us operate Grubs Up, including:

  • Supabase, for authentication, database hosting, server functions, and file storage;
  • AI processing providers, when needed to support recipe import and OCR-related features; and
  • Google Play and platform providers, to distribute and support the app.

If you enable a recipe sharing feature, recipe information included in that share may be accessible to anyone with the share link or token.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as it is needed to provide the app and operate your account, unless a longer retention period is required for legal, security, or operational reasons.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect the information we store. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices

You can generally:

  • update recipe, meal-planning, and household information within the app;
  • remove content you no longer want to keep;
  • choose whether to upload photos, files, or recipe content; and
  • choose whether to use optional sharing features.

Children's Privacy

Grubs Up is not directed to children and is not intended for use by children without a parent or guardian's involvement.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we may update the date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, provide additional notice in the app.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please use the contact details listed on the Grubs Up Google Play store listing or the support channel provided inside the app.
