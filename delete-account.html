Delete your Grubs Up account

Last updated: July 19, 2026

If you would like us to delete your Grubs Up account and associated app data, you can request deletion using the steps below.

Request deletion by email:
Send an email to grubsup.support@gmail.com with the subject Delete my Grubs Up account.

What to include

  • the email address used for your Grubs Up account;
  • your display name, if available; and
  • any details that help us identify the correct account safely.

What we delete

When your request is verified and processed, we aim to delete the following from the active Grubs Up service:

  • your Grubs Up account record;
  • household profile information;
  • saved recipes and recipe images you uploaded;
  • meal plans and shopping lists; and
  • other account-linked app content associated with normal app use.

What may be retained briefly

We may retain limited records for a short period where reasonably necessary for security, fraud prevention, debugging, legal compliance, or to complete the deletion request itself.

How long it takes

We aim to process verified deletion requests within 7 days of receiving them, although some cases may take longer if we need additional information to confirm account ownership.

Need help instead?

If you are having trouble signing in or want help before deleting your account, contact grubsup.support@gmail.com.
